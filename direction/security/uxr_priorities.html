Overview

We regularly perform UX research to better understand the needs of our users and to also learn how those users interact with our product. This prioritized list shows the areas of UX research that are highest in priority for the Sec section product team.

Product Priorities

Priority Name Group
1 Understand Important Vulnerability Metrics and Security Dashboard Requirements Threat Insights
2 Exploring Compliance Framework Label Limits Security Policies & Compliance
3 Critical Compliance Frameworks & Regulatory Requirements for GitLab Customers Compliance
4 Consolidating Roles and Permissions - Grouping & CRUD validation Authorization
5 Security Adoption Barriers Study All - Applies to Secure and Govern
6 Secure & Govern Notification and Alerting Needs Security Policies
7 Passkey Solution Validation Authentication
