Overview

The next GitLab release we are kicking off is 17.4 and this page contains the list of issues that are currently planned for that release. We present this during the monthly kickoff call, which is publicly streamed to the GitLab Unfiltered YouTube channel.

This meeting is hosted by the Chief Product Officer, or a member of the Product Leadership Team. During the meeting, Product Section leaders will present highlights from their respective areas. We will not cover each item in the list below, so feel free to click on any you're interested in and participate directly in the issue. Please also use YouTube comments instead of Google Hangout or Zoom comments.

This page updates every month to show the next release we will be kicking off. We also have a YouTube playlist of the below referenced recorded Kickoffs.
