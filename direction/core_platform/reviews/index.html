What's on this page

This page contains the bi-weekly Core Platform & SaaS Platforms section direction review. Every other week, the PM teams reviews one of the groups direction pages asynchronously and adds their questions to the weekly PM agenda discussion. During the weekly meeting, these questions are discussed synchronously and recorded separately from the main meeting.
