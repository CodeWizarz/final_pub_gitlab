  4. HOW TO ENTER THE PROMOTIONAL GAMES:

    • Enter the Ethics & Compliance Trivia Quiz during the Promotion Period online by:
    • Completing the form which includes the Trivia Quiz, located at: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1Jm53Vp40Bz5Ng6VNadas-e1b_VSwnhwRbT7P5CDL3x4/viewform?edit_requested=true. Check the box to record your GitLab email address which appears on the form, as the email to be included in your response.
    • Complete the Trivia Quiz and click the “Submit” button.
    • Entrants who receive a perfect score on the Trivia Quiz will be entered into a drawing for a chance to win one (1) of three (3) prizes, as described in Section 3.
    • Enter the Ethics & Compliance Scavenger Hunt during the Promotion Period online by:
    • Completing the form which includes the Scavenger Hunt questions, located at: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1DYaSBdbHuE9REOdMyVix0EZWfiO5irjdRZHCFXUCFYo/viewform?edit_requested=true. Check the box to record your GitLab email address which appears on the form, as the email to be included in your response.
    • Complete the Scavenger Hunt questions and click the “Submit” button.
    • Entrants who provide an answer to every question will be entered into a drawing for a chance to win one (1) of three (3) prizes.

    ENTRANTS ARE NOT OBLIGATED TO PARTICIPATE IN BOTH PROMOTIONAL GAMES. Automated or robotic Entries submitted by individuals or organizations will be disqualified. Internet entry must be made by the Entrant. Any attempt by Entrant to obtain more than the stated number of Entries by using multiple/different email addresses, identities, registrations, logins or any other methods, including, but not limited to, commercial contest/Promotional Game subscription notification and/or entering services, will void Entrant's Entries and that Entrant may be disqualified. Final eligibility for the award of any prize is subject to eligibility verification as set forth below. All Entries must be posted by the end of the Promotion Period in order to participate. Sponsor's database clock will be the official timekeeper for this Promotional Game.
