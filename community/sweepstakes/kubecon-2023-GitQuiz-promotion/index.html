4. HOW TO ENTER: Enter the Promotional Game during the Promotion Period by visiting the GitLab M1 booth at the KubeCon NA 2023 conference, scheduled to take place in Chicago, Illinois, USA, between 7 November 2023 and 9 November 2023, and follow the instructions below:

  • Go to the GitLab booth during the days and times noted below, and tell a GitLab representative at the booth that you would like to participate in the AI trivia game.
  • The GitLab representative will take the names of up to the first ten (10) participants who are present during the Start Time and End Times on the days noted below, and those ten (10) will be eligible to participate ("Entrants"). Entrants must be present in the GitLab booth during the Start Time and End Times in order to participate.
DAY START TIME END TIME
07 November 2023 11:00 AM CST 11:15 AM CST
08 November 2023 3:00 PM CST 3:15 PM CST
09 November 2023 11:00 AM CST 11:15 AM CST
  • The GitLab representative will provide the Entrants with a QR code and/or a PIN code to access the trivia game on the Kahoot! platform, and will provide instructions on accessing the Kahoot! platform via the Entrants mobile device. YOU MUST HAVE A MOBILE DEVICE TO PARTICIPATE. Entrants are free to sign up on the Kahoot! platform.
  • Once on the Kahoot! Platform, entrants will enter a screen name to have access to the trivia game.
  • Entrants will take the trivia quiz, and the results will appear on a screen in the GitLab booth.
  • A GitLab representative will call out the screen name with the highest score within ten (10) minutes after the End Times. Entrants must be present at the GitLab booth.

Automated or robotic Entries submitted by individuals or organizations will be disqualified. Internet entry must be made by the Entrant. Any attempt by Entrant to obtain more than the stated number of Entries by using multiple/different email addresses, identities, registrations, logins or any other methods, including, but not limited to, commercial contest/Promotional Game subscription notification and/or entering services, will void Entrant's Entries and that Entrant may be disqualified. Final eligibility for the award of any prize is subject to eligibility verification as set forth below. All Entries must be posted by the end of the Promotion Period in order to participate. Sponsor's database clock will be the official timekeeper for this Promotional Game.

5. WINNER SELECTION: Entrants who receive the highest number of correct responses to the Trivia Quiz, out of the ten (10) participants who entered the Promotional Game for that day, will be deemed the winner for the Promotional Game on that day. In the event of a tie, each winning Entrant will receive the USD $20.00 gift card prize and will have a chance to spin the prize wheel at the booth for one (1) GitLab branded prize. Odds of winning will vary depending on the number of eligible Entries received.
